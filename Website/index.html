About Fancy Cakes

So you may question your self what is Fancy Cakes? Well look no further! If you're looking to order a cake for any occasion whether it be a birthday, wedding, anniversary, you name we will make it for you. Fancy cakes has been going strong for the past 3 years, with excellent customer opinions. Every cake is precisely made, to make each and every customer happy with their order.

About me

My name is Marta, I'm originally from Poland but I've lived in England for the past 10 years. I've been making cakes for family for 4 years and stared making them for others 3 years ago. Making cakes is my hobby, I really enjoy making them and put 100% into each and everyone of my cakes to make sure that they are the best they can possibly be.
